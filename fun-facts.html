Earthquake Fun Facts

Interesting facts about earthquakes from around the world.

Fun Facts

Largest Earthquake
The largest earthquake ever recorded was 9.5 magnitude in Chile, 1960.
Most Earthquake-Prone Country
Japan experiences frequent earthquakes due to tectonic plate activity.
Deepest Recorded Earthquake
Approximately 700 km below the surface in Fiji.
Smallest Detectable Earthquake
Seismographs can detect earthquakes smaller than magnitude 1.0.
Fastest Seismic Waves
P-waves travel up to 8 km/s through Earth's interior.
