Earthquake Prevention & Safety Measures

Learn practical steps to stay safe before, during, and after an earthquake.

Earthquake Safety Tips

Secure Furniture
Secure heavy furniture, appliances, and wall mountings to prevent falling hazards.
Emergency Kit
Keep an emergency kit with water, torch, first-aid, and radio ready for quick access.
Family Plan
Prepare a family evacuation and communication plan so everyone knows where to go.
Safe Spots
Identify safe spots in each room like under tables or against walls away from windows.
Drop, Cover, Hold On
Protect your head and neck, stay low, and hold on to sturdy furniture.
Go Outdoors Safely
If outdoors, move to an open area away from buildings, trees, and power lines.
Avoid Hazards
Avoid elevators and unsecured objects that may fall during shaking.
Stay Calm
Remain calm and follow your pre-planned safety protocol to reduce panic.
Check Injuries
Check yourself and others for injuries and provide first aid if necessary.
Inspect Surroundings
Inspect your surroundings for structural damage and potential hazards.
Turn Off Gas
Turn off gas if you suspect a leak and avoid using open flames.
Be Prepared
Be ready for aftershocks and follow instructions from local authorities.
